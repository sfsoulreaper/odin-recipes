Girl-Dog Plushie

Description

A 4-year old girl who loves her dog and father.

Ingredients:

  • A loving father.
  • A Pyrenean Mountain Dog
  • An innocent 4 or 5-year old daughter.
  • Transmutation circle.

Instructions:

  1. Tell your daugher how much you love her.
  2. Draw the transmutation circle as illustrated.
  3. Transmute the dog and the daugher inside the transmutation circle. Note: You don't need an equivalent exchange for this as the girl already has a soul of its own.
  4. If it speaks, then it is a success. Enjoy.
